# F7: Smart Shopping List

> **Status:** Phase 2 — chua implement

## Mo ta

Tu dong tao danh sach mua sam tu meal plan da len lich, giup nguoi dung tiet kiem thoi gian va khong bo sot nguyen lieu.

## Features planned

- **Auto-generate tu meal plan:** Chon meal plan → he thong trich xuat tat ca nguyen lieu can thiet
- **Gop nguyen lieu trung:** 2 mon can hanh → gop tong so luong (vi du: 3 cu hanh)
- **Phan loai theo khu vuc sieu thi:** Rau cu, thit ca, gia vi, do kho, dong lanh...
- **Check-off khi mua xong:** Danh dau tung item da mua, theo doi tien do
- **Tich hop dat hang online:** Ket noi GrabMart, Shopee Fresh de dat hang truc tiep
- **Uoc tinh chi phi:** Tinh toan chi phi du kien dua tren gia tham khao

## Ghi chu

Spec chi tiet se duoc bo sung truoc khi bat dau implement Phase 2.
